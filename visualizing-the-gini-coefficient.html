Gini coefficient:
The Gini coefficient is an estimator of dispersion of a statistical population, calculated using a random sample.

The above definition seems satisfying at first, but if you think about it there are many estimators that fit the description.

  • Standard Deviation
  • Interquartile range

Maybe we should quit hiding the equations behind it.

Gini coefficient:
$ 1 - 2 \times \int\limits_0^1 L(X) dX $
